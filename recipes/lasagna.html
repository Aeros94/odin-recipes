Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an italian dish consisting of layers of cheese and a tomato based meatsauce and the noodle it gets it's namesake from.

It has it's roots in Italy, but lasagna has spread to many corners of the world. I'll be showing you the version i've been making for my family.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles..............1 250 gram pack
  • Marinara...........................5 cups
  • Ground Sausage.............. 2 cups
  • Bechamel..........................1 cup

Directions

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. While your pasta is boiling, preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Prepare your bechamel sauce.
  4. Assemble your layers following this pattern:
    • Sauce
    • Noodles
    • Cheese
  5. Once assembled bake for 30 minutes, turning halfway through.
  6. Serve while hot. If refrigerating, let cool completely then wrap in plastic wrap.
